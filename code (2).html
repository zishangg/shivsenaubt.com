About Us

Our Mission

Shiv Sena (UBT) is committed to upholding the ideals of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and fighting for the rights of Marathi people. Under the leadership of Shri Uddhav Thackeray, we work tirelessly for the betterment of Maharashtra and its people.

Vinod Pawar's Work

As City Head, Vinod Pawar has led numerous initiatives for local development, youth empowerment, and social welfare. His dedication to public service has brought positive change to our community.
